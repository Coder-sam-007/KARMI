Forest & Environment

Forest coverage area has been declining continuously. To meet the daily need of forest and conserve the natural resources KARMI has been taken forest protection as a massive programme by formation of JFM and CFM in the potential villages. In the depleted forest area we have taken initiative to plantation programme by proving seeding from our nursery and cooperate with the Govt. forest department. KARMI believes without networking and collaboration preservation of natural resource is too difficulties. To keep close collaboration and create opportunity to create block level or district level forest committee. We organized workshops on forest protection and collaborating to Govt. through joint forest management and community forest management by formation of Vana Surakhya Samiti. Local Ranger and Forest Officers has been invited to these workshops and helps the committees and networking proper guide and support on forest protection and formation of JFM &CFM.
