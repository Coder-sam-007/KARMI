About Us

Kalahandi Organisation for Agriculture and Rural Marketing Initiative (KARMI) “Kalahandi Organization for Agriculture and Rural Marketing Initiative (KARMI) came in to existence in the year 1995. Form at by some energetic youths with the humble motive to help the poor, understand the dehumanization process in the society and become conscious of their own self and take the appropriate action for developing the life quality of the tribal and downtrodden especially the woman and children.

By analyzing the socio-economic and political problem of the society and promotion of agriculture the main livelihood of Kalahandi as a prime chapter with agriculture and rural marketing growth to help people and development awareness and education but subsequently community capacity building and sustainable health improvement also considered as a main motive of the organization. To make Kalahandi as model to utilize the recourses stop distress migration, hunger and starvation death. The mission of KARMI as to undertake, promote or organization socio welfare educational and developmental activities aim at living condition and generate socio , economic , civil and moral conditions of the people particularly of weaker sections of the community without discrimination of cases , creed and religion.

The journey of KARMI was started from Golamunda block of Kalahandi district with support of Agragamee one of the leading organization of Odisha to promote the tribal and rural development and than become KARMI will be a independent organization since 1997. In a nutshell, the struggle against issues was the struggle for Socio- Economic rebirth of the community. The focal group of the organization are scheduled tribe, scheduled caste, women, children, landless laborers, rural artisans and other weaker sections of the society. KARMI began its intensive effort and various initiatives, systematic effort to identify the real causes & opposed to spread of several myths about distress & deprivation in the operational areas opened a new chapter for people’s action. Some committed youth were united together, Through their sincere and active cooperation Yubak Sangha and Nari Sangha were formed.

Our Aim :

To establish a just, sustainable and participatory society by utilising the local available natural resources and services where each individual will live with dignity and self respect.

Objectives :

KARMI has been functioning with appropriate programme indigenous style of implementation and generate a greater level of people participation.

  • To undertake, promote or organize social welfare, educational and development activities aimed at improving the living condition and generate social, economic, civil, and moral conditions of the people particularly of the weaker section of the community without distinction of caste, creed, and religion.
  • To educate and assist the rural poor to release their creative faculties for their economic, health, social and cultural improvement.
  • To bring awareness among a target group of scheduled tribes and other underprivileged groups such as scheduled caste , small and marginal farmers landless, and bonded labors for self reliance and social justice .
  • To establish a training based for the grass root functionary drawn from the target group as well as the young educated professionals. The training would cover education , agriculture , health , skill improvement and income generation activities.
  • To undertake research studies relevant to the target group with the aim of searching alternative development strategies.
  • KARMI has strived for and actualized people participation in many areas of its works and is pound to stay that it dreams much of its strength from the process.
  • Self-reliance is the autonomous capacity to take decision on one’s own livelihood and chose ones course of development. It means the ability to assess over ones means of livelihood and ones environment, participation as a central democratic value is organically linked with this ability for its means organized efforts to increase control over resources and instituting on the part of the people who have neither to have been excluded from such control. Liberation from domination and exploitation require a measure of counter power against the dominant interest with the community.

Structure :

KARMI ‘s structure is basically built upon the strong foundation-keeping people and village as the center of the focus. The problems identification, planning process, the appraisal of the resources, the implementation, monitoring, the evolution and above all the accountability starts with the people /organization. The village committees are instrumental in taking key decision. The animators, Shrimik Sathi, volunteers and the community health workers area the king pin the process of action reflection relation to his, her own village and neighbor villages facilitate the decision making of the village committees.

The activities the village committees and animators are controlled by the field centers. The field/cluster is manage by a field/cluster coordinators who has the training and orientation to establish a good rapport and a dynamic communication with the animators and village committee to initiate a people to organization process and ensure their full participation in the implementation and monitoring. All the field center/cluster office located strategically to reach out to the most backward pocket of the project and take responsibility. Now KARMI operates its activities in three projects as Golamunda and M.Rampur blocks of Kalahandi district. The head office of KARMI is situated at Mahaling in Goalmunda block of Kalahandi district and a project office as well the Coordination Office at M.Ramapur in M.Rampur block.

All the project coordinators are the in charge of the block /Project having two or three field coordinators who are in charge of the G.Ps . All the field coordinators are also supported to provide two ways linkages with the animators/community and the programme coordinator. The programme coordinators are the in charge of implement all the programme. The secretary guides the programme coordinator. Secretary of KARMI legally responsible for the management of KARMI. And the Secretary is the secretary of the Governing Body and General Body has a lot of constitutional responsibility like producing Annual Report and the Progress Report and utilization certificates of the projects implemented by KARMI in time.

Programmes Implimented so far by KARMI :


(A) Education :

  • Non-formal and Adult education for non-literate people in the age group of 15 to 35 years.
  • Accelerated total women literacy project covers 9000 woman in the age group of 15 to 35 years with support of Ministry of Human Resource Development Govt. of India & National Literacy Mission.
  • Qualitative Tribal Education for Tribal Children in the age group of 5 to 12 years where there is no Govt. school or other educational facility covered 560 children with support of Indo-German Social Service Society (IGSSS) New Delhi.
  • Library Programme : KARMI continue its library programme to in reach the education facility to children, New literate Adult & Women, Researchers and Academician cover with support of Rajaram Mohan Library Foundation through Department of Culture, Govt. of Orissa.
  • Residential Care Centre for the boys and girls of migrants who have gone to other states for getting employment .KARMI undertake 10 residential care center in most migrant prone villages and 400 student are staying there with education and health facility with support of OPEPA, and American Indian Foundation (AIF) U.S.A.
  • Educational exposure, visit, exhibition, popularization of science among the rural children and organize Balmela, Children fair to educate the children of primary & upper primary level

(B) Agriculture, Marketing and Livelihood :

  • Formation of grain bank, community fund and its proper management for insure food security of poor, marginalized and target groups.
  • Promotion of 150 self help groups and trained with management, fit with different trades after vocational training covers 3000 women of needs with support of Mission Shakti, National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) Build Co-operative and linkage with Govt. Financial Institutions & Banks.
  • Through formation of Farmer's Club KARMI organized farmers training on agriculture, horticulture, pisciculture and vocational training, management of Natural Resources as forest protection, soil conservation and land and water management with the concept of watershed with support of Council for Advancement of People's Action and Rural Technology, (CAPART) Department of Rural Development Govt, of India.
  • Constructed 2 Diversion based irrigation project with support of Sir Dorabji Tata Trust and provide irrigation and drinking water facility, develop backyard firm, and kitchen garden in 2 villages in Golamunda block of Kalahandi district.
  • Undertakes Migration programme for imporveing the condition of Migrant workers of Golamunda and M.Rampur block and provide ID facility, helath, legal and financial improvement, skill building and placement in destination and cover 11000 migrant workers family.

(C) Community Capacity Building :

  • Formation and empowerment of issue based community based organizations in village and cluster level and build up a strong networking with the Regional Resource Center and Para Legal-workers programme.
  • Empowerment of women, tribal and rural farmers on literacy, legal right advocacy through women resource center, protection of human right, legal aid and legal literacy programme.
  • Promotion of SHG, Co-operative and linkage the rural market with other big institutions through empower Farmers Club and vocational trainings with support of NABARD, Mission Sakti, and Utkal Gramya Bank

(D) Health :

  • Health awareness among the women, rural poor and develop the community health through sanitation programme, kitchen gardening and immunization.
  • Bare-foot health manager and community health clinic management working 10 bear foot health manager in remote inaccessible pocket of Golamunda Block of Kalahandi District, where Govt. health service could not reach, Reproductive Child Health Programme in remote pocket, Promotion of kitchen gardening ,farmers Training, and TBA Programme with support MISEREOR, Germany since 2005.
  • AIDS awareness and control programme for Migrant labor, adult cense boys & girls and sex workers and settlement through IGP & SHG.

Achievements of KARMI in Last 3 Years :


(A) Health programme :

In the last phrase of the project “Community health management in Golamunda block of Kalahandi district “ project supported by MISERIOR, Germany KARMI has been able to achieve the following achievements. The health awareness and health education among the target people has developed through conduct 340 village Health awareness Camps which covered 41 villages in 8 G.P. of Golamunda block covers 30103 people.

  • All the immunization camps conducted by Govt. personal with support of our barefoot health worker and Coordinator make it 100% successful.
  • the Health camps organized by KARMI peasants has been diagnosis on different diseases and got medical treatment yet 20 medical camps has been organized and total 4360 peasants have been diagnosis and able to treatment their diseases in ease way.
  • 15 nos. of bear foot health workers has been trained and posted inaccessible pocket and they coves 3 to 5 villages each and deal ting with community health and hygiene. They also involve on governmental programme to make it successful and provide door step medicine at the time of need.
  • The kitchen gardening programme involves 192 nos. of farmers in 32 villages and the vegetable cultivation also increased by 50% during this period. Which able to check mal-nutrition and increases the income of small and marginal farmers.
  • Barefoot health workers with our Co-coordinators also trying their level best to in reach the Govt. Programme to the people as ICDS, NMEP, Total Sanitation campaign, Leprosy eradication programme, etc.
  • Popularization of herbal medicine by the Baiday’s (Traditional Medicine Practitioner) through training and workshop

(B) Tribal Education :

In the project supported by National Education Group New-Delhi” Qualitative Tribal Educating project in Inaccessible pocket of Golamunda Block of Kalahandi District”. By this project in KARMI covered only 10 villages and the total population of the villages are 5324 and the total nos. of students in the age group of 6 to 14 is 605 by 4 new innovative schools and 6 Govt. primary school which are not functioning properly here KARMI undertook the Education project for the period of 3 years and took steps to decrease the dropout rate and properly functioned the government primary school. By the project in 3 years we organized 190 village meetings where total 10640 villagers attained those meeting and got awareness on education and ensure participation of good and qualitative education. Total no of 456 students got qualitative education and check the dropout rate and 247 students had been admitted to the upper primary school,. After competition of the project the villagers also continuing the innovative school in their own fund . In the project period we conducted 12 nos. of teachers training where 15 teachers got regular qualitative training and improving the teaching quality. The teaching and learning material provided by the project help the poor students to continue their study and encourages them in the learning. The programme has develop a critical look of the common man towards formal school teachers and their activities.
