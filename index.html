Who are we ?

KARMI is a Non-Governmental, Non-political, Voluntary Organisation that has been working in Kalahandi District of Odisha since 1997 with the objectives to bring sustainable change in the socio-economic condition of rural, tribal, disadvantaged and oppressed masses in the community with a group of experience and trained social workers.

The office of the organization is situated at Kadobhatta village of Mahaling GP in Golamunda block and Coordination office situated in M.Rampur and other project offices in concerned block. KARMI has been registered under the Society Registration Act1860, FCRA by Ministry of Home Affairs, Govt. of India and Income Tax Act 12A and 80G.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission:

Sustainable Societal development / change through capacitating the weaker sections/ poor/ oppressed and their institutions by organizing training, exposure, workshop, seminar, study & research with greater emphasis on involvement & participation for educational, socio - economic , political & moral development with gendered in built bottom - up PME process.

Vision:

A changed society with more social peace gender equity & justice , in favour of the poorest and the weaker section of the society irrespective of Caste, Creed and Gender.
